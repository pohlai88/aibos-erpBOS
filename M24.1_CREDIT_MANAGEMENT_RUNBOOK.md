# M24.1 — Credit Limits, Auto-Hold/Release & Collections Workbench Runbook

## Overview

M24.1 is a tight, incremental add-on to M24 that closes the loop from **risk → collect**. It introduces customer credit policies, automatic order/invoice holds when risk spikes, guided collections, and instant releases once risk recedes or payment clears. Zero churn to existing M24 entities.

## Features

- **Credit policy & limits** per customer/segment with risk score inputs
- **Auto-hold engine** on **new invoices / open orders** when limits or DSO rules breach
- **Release rules** tied to **cash application** (M24) and **PTP** adherence
- **Collections Workbench** APIs: prioritized call lists, KPIs, notes, and contact log
- **Forecast bridge**: risk status nudges M22 receipts confidence

## Architecture

### Database Schema

The system adds 6 new tables to M24:

1. `ar_credit_policy` - Credit policy configuration
2. `ar_customer_credit` - Customer credit limits and overrides
3. `ar_credit_hold_log` - Hold/release event audit trail
4. `ar_collections_note` - Collections activity notes
5. `ar_collections_kpi` - Daily KPI snapshots
6. Performance indexes for optimization

### Services

- **ArCreditManagementService**: Core credit evaluation and workbench functionality
- **Exposure Calculator**: Real-time exposure and DSO calculation
- **Auto-Hold Engine**: Automated hold/release based on policy breaches
- **Collections Workbench**: Prioritized customer lists and activity tracking

### API Endpoints

- `GET/POST /api/ar/credit/policies` - Credit policy management
- `GET/POST /api/ar/credit/customers` - Customer credit overrides
- `POST /api/ar/credit/evaluate` - Run credit evaluation (dry-run option)
- `GET /api/ar/collect/workbench` - Prioritized collections queue
- `POST /api/ar/collect/notes` - Add collections notes
- `GET /api/ar/collect/kpi` - Generate KPI snapshots

## Business Logic

### Auto-Hold Triggers

Customers are automatically placed on hold when:

1. **Exposure > Credit Limit**: Total exposure exceeds assigned credit limit
2. **DSO > Target + Grace**: Days Sales Outstanding exceeds target plus grace period
3. **Risk Score Breach**: Risk score × risk weight > 0.7 threshold
4. **PTP Tolerance**: Number of open Promise-to-Pay exceeds tolerance limit

### Auto-Release Conditions

Customers are automatically released from hold when:

1. **No Breaches**: All policy conditions are met
2. **No Recent Broken PTPs**: No broken Promise-to-Pay in last 7 days
3. **Significant Payment**: Recent cash application covers >50% of exposure

### Collections Workbench Priority

Customers are prioritized by:

1. **Priority Score**: Exposure × (1 + DSO/30) - higher is more urgent
2. **Bucket**: Older buckets (90+) rank higher
3. **Hold Status**: On-hold customers get priority
4. **Next Action**: Customers with overdue next actions

## Monitoring & Alerts

### Key Metrics

- **Total Exposure**: Sum of all customer exposures
- **Customers on Hold**: Number of customers currently on hold
- **Average DSO**: Company-wide Days Sales Outstanding
- **Hold Rate**: Percentage of customers on hold
- **Release Rate**: Percentage of holds released daily
